## Guard Added

Added a local pre-commit hook `no-root-object-artifacts` that fails when any of
the following files appear at the repository root:

- `*.o`
- `*.a`
- `*.so`
- `*.dylib`

The hook points users to `git clean -fdX` or moving artifacts under `build/`.
